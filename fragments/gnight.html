Dreams are the expression of everything that's on your mind — they are weird, hard to understand, and often forgotten.

Your Visual Dream Journal

The dream timeline shows the user the people, places and things they have dreamed about recently. The user can zoom in on the timeline and interact with the dreams and characters. They can view the dream transcripts, or navigate to the specific character pages.

Statistics

The statistics feature of the website shows the user patterns and trends between their length of sleep, the vividness of their dreams, and the mood of their dreams.

Dream Catalog

The dream catalog serves as documentation of all the dreams the user has logged. The user can view the transcripts of all the dreams, so this acts as a digital dream journal. Additionally, this is where users are able to upload images to be used in the timeline and collages.
